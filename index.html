Retail Stock Market Analyzer

End-to-end data pipeline analyzing 5 years of historical stock data across Target, Walmart, Costco, Kroger, and Ulta Beauty. Ingests raw market data via Python, loads into BigQuery, and runs advanced SQL analytics including moving averages, annualized volatility, beta calculations, and momentum signals using window functions. Built a Random Forest classifier with scikit-learn to predict daily price direction, with predictions stored back in BigQuery. Fully documented and version controlled on GitHub.
